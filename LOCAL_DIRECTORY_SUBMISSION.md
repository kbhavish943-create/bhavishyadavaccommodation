## 💡 PRO TIPS

### 1. **Consistency is KEY**
Use EXACT same information on all directories:
- Business name
- Address (spell exactly same way)
- Phone number
- Website
- Description (at least first 100 words same)

**Why?** Google uses this for local ranking signals. Inconsistency = lower rankings.

### 2. **Use Professional Photos**
Upload 10+ high-quality photos showing:
- Room interior (different types)
- Study area
- Kitchen
- Bathroom
- Parking
- Entrance/exterior
- WiFi setup
- Security/CCTV

**Impact:** 10x more inquiries with good photos

### 3. **Write Compelling Descriptions**
- Use keywords naturally (Boys hostel, student accommodation, PG)
- Highlight unique selling points
- Mention target students (college, UPSC, NEET, etc.)
- Include pricing
- Add contact information clearly

### 4. **Respond to All Reviews**
Once you get reviews:
- Reply to ALL reviews (good & bad)
- Thank customers for positive reviews
- Address concerns in negative reviews
- Show you care about customer satisfaction

### 5. **Update Regularly**
- Add new photos quarterly
- Update available rooms/prices
- Keep hours & contact info current
- Share updates/special offers

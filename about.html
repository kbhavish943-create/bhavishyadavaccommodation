About Us

Meet the founder and discover the story behind BHAVISH YADAV ACCOMMODATION LODGE

About Bhavish Yadav

Bhavish Yadav is a dedicated professional with a strong academic foundation and a passion for serving students through quality accommodation services. He completed his B.Sc. in Mathematics (Honours) and is currently pursuing an MBA while preparing for the UPSC examination, reflecting his commitment to continuous learning and personal growth.

Alongside his academic journey, Bhavish is the Founder and Owner of Bhavish Yadav Accommodations Lodge, a trusted and student-friendly PG accommodation located in the heart of Deoghar, Jharkhand, India. His mission is to provide a comfortable and safe living environment for students who come from outside the region to pursue their education.

About the Lodge

Bhavish Yadav Accommodations Lodge is strategically located in Baijnathpur, Bilasi, Deoghar, Jharkhand, India. The lodge is designed to provide a comfortable, safe, and convenient living experience for students coming from outside the region.
